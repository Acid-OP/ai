Investment methodology
This portfolio is constructed using principles of Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT), which seeks to optimize risk-adjusted returns through broad diversification across various asset classes. Similar to methodologies popularized by platforms like Wealthfront, we emphasize global diversification to mitigate specific market risks and achieve stable growth. This approach focuses on building a resilient portfolio designed for capital preservation and modest returns.
  • Prioritizes capital preservation and low volatility.
  • Globally diversified across equities, bonds, and real estate.
  • Utilizes cost-effective Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs).
  • Strategic asset allocation tailored for shorter time horizons.
